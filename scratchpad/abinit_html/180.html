4     !!  abi_xgemm
5     !!
6     !! FUNCTION
7     !!  abi_xgemm is the generic function that solve :
8     !! *     C := alpha*op( A )*op( B ) + beta*C,
9     !! *
10     !! *  where  op( X ) is one of
11     !! *
12     !! *     op( X ) = X   or   op( X ) = X**T,
13     !! *
14     !! *  alpha and beta are scalars, and A, B and C are matrices, with op( A )
15     !! *  an m by k matrix,  op( B )  a  k by n matrix and  C an m by n matrix.
